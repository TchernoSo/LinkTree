My Soul

Meant To Be
This is my journey for more than 15 years of self-education, discipline, and consistency.
Life itself has been my greatest teacher and taught me the importance of embracing the journey with open arms.
Sports showed me the values of discipline and teamwork, Art increased my creativity and imagination and Tech taught me the ability to innovate and optimize. Today I am who I am because of the choices I made, my choices created my path.

Tcherno So
Dear Soul.
Tcherno here, an entirely Self-Taught Artist.
00's
Born in the Netherlands, moved to Portugal at the age of two.
My mother remembers how I always carried the ball with me, refusing to leave it behind, as it was the only thing I brought from home.
I would spend hours kicking the ball against the wall and playing alone in the streets until I started to be surrounded by amazing friends, and a few years later I joined my first amateur football club.
Just 4 years later, I suffered an ACL injury, leading to surgery and six to eight months of recovery. This resulted in a decrease of my confidence and fitness, so I decided to take a break. (Never knew I'd come back!)
2016
Art was starting to develop in my soul and I needed a challenge, something to push me further, so I decided to learn HipHop dance and months later, I quit my job, sold my car, and left Lisbon, leaving behind my family and friends to move to London, a city renowned for its art scene, creativity, and diversity. I am thankful for the journey and the amazing people I have met.
I joined a skilled team who helped me grow as a dancer and together we created unforgettable performances in London for four years.
2020
The pandemic!
Personally, the loneliest moment of my life, a period of deep reflection!
End up feeling like something good came out of something bad. This period has revealed my true essence. I am stronger, more aware, and infinitely more at peace.
Difficult times can lead us to a deeper understanding of who we are and what we are capable of. Since then, I have been writing and dedicated to creating a world where no one has to face challenges alone "The Soul".
2022
I decided to create my website.
After research and questions, I decided to begin the journey of studying computer science and programming. From my first line of code to building this website from scratch. Day by day, I found myself increasingly fascinated with the magic of code, the power of algorithms, and the capability to generate something out of nothing. Coding for me is like a limitless game, with constant upgrades but never an end. I will continue on this journey and see what the future holds.
