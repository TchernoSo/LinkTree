Hi, I'm Tcherno, an artist and football enthusiast, and I am entirely self-taught.

I'm a dance instructor, life coach and programmer since 2022.
Along my incredible journey, I've been blessed enough to cross paths with countless people across europe, each one taught me something unique and special. I've been dancing since 2016 alongside a highly experienced crew, where I had the chance to boost my growth and evolution, as we worked together to create unforgettable performances acroos the vibrant city of London, UK for 4 years.

Unfortunately, the pandemic changed our lives, including mine. I discovered a newfound passion for life coaching, which might come as a surprise, believe it or not, it's during tough times we gain something special, leading to a deeper understanding of who we are and what we are capable of, because we are pushed to our limits and forced to confront our strengths, weaknesses, and deepest desires. I started this journey when I needed to overcome a challenging period in my life, clear my mind, eliminate the fear and self-doubt, set new goals, and be consistent, focusing only on what's truly important to me... and a few months later I did, I made it and I'll keep going.

Recently, I've been delving into the fascinating world of computer science and programming, embarking on a new learning adventure. This pursuit has opened up exciting new possibilities and has brought me to where I am today. The fusion of my diverse passions, from dance to life coaching to programming, has shaped me into the person I am today, and I'm excited to see where this multifaceted path takes me in the future.
