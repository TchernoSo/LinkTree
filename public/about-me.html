Heart + Mind = Soul

Welcome Soul, Tcherno here, an entirely artist self-taught.

At age 2, I moved from the Netherlands to Portugal with a passion for football. A ball and a goal were all I needed to be happy. But in 2014, my dreams of becoming a professional football player were shattered when I suffered a devastating knee ligament injury (ACL) that required surgery.

In 2016, I left Lisbon for London, eager to pursue my passion for dance and challenge myself to learn and grow in a different environment. Luckily I had the chance to join a highly experienced crew, where I met incredible people who supported my evolution as a dancer. Together, we created unforgettable performances across the vibrant city for four years.

In 2020, the pandemic changed our lives, including mine, during that period in order to guide myself through a difficult moment on my own, I started exploring the world of life coaching, which didn't come as a surprise considering my family and friends had previously observed that on me, but I always chose to focus in my other passions, like football, but tough times, lead us to a deeper understanding of who we really are and what we are capable of.
After learning about the concept of life and its challenges, I successfully disciplined myself and transformed into a better person. Since then, I have been helping others in similar situations

In 2022, I challenged myself to develop a new skill: learning computer science and programming. This pursuit has opened up exciting new possibilities, such as building this website entirely from scratch, and brought me to where I am today.

This is my journey of self-taught, discipline and consistency, for over 15 years.
The fusion of my passions and unimaginable journey, from sports to dance to life coaching to programming, has shaped me into a better version of myself for sure and I'm excited to see where this multifaceted path takes me in the future.
