Hi, I'm Tcherno, an artist and football enthusiast, and I am entirely self-taught.

A dance instructor, life coach and programmer since 2022.
My incredible journey, blessed me with countless people across Europe, where each one taught me something special.

Been dancing since 2016 alongside a highly experienced crew, where I had the chance to boost my growth and evolution, as we worked together creating unforgettable performances acroos the vibrant city of London, UK for over 4 years.

Unfortunately, in 2020, the pandemic changed our lives, including mine, during that period, which didn't come as a surprise considering my family and friends had previously observed it in me, but I always choose instead to focus on pursuing my true passions, it's during tough times we gain something special, leading to a deeper understanding of who we really are and what we are capable of, because we are pushed to our limits and forced to confront our weaknesses and deepest desires.
So I started this journey when I needed to overcome a challenging period of my life, clear my mind, eliminate the fear and self-doubt, set new goals, and be consistent, focusing only on what's truly important to me, and I have successfully obtained my certification from the Universal Coach Institute.

Since 2022, I've been delving into the fascinating world of computer science and programming, embarking on a new learning adventure. This pursuit has opened up exciting new possibilities and has brought me to where I am today. The fusion of my diverse passions, from dance to life coaching to programming, has shaped me into a better version of myself and I'm excited to see where this multifaceted path takes me in the future.
