Protect your Soul

Hi, my name is Tcherno, an artist, dance instructor, life coach and programmer and I am entirely self-taught.

On my journey, I had the opportunity to meet people across the world, where each one taught me something unique.

I was born in the Netherlands, spent my entire life in Portugal, and am currently residing in the United Kingdom.
Starting from dance, been dancing since 2016 alongside a highly experienced crew, where I had the chance to boost my growth and evolution as we worked together, creating unforgettable performances acroos the vibrant city of London for 4 years. "Artist"

Unfortunately, in 2020, the pandemic changed our lives, including mine, during that period in order to guide myself through one of the most difficult phases I've experienced, I started exploring the world of life coaching, which didn't come as a surprise considering my family and friends had previously observed that on me, but I always chose to focus on pursuing my other passions, like football, I always dreamed to become a professional football player, where I invested 8 years, but tough times, lead us to a deeper understanding of who we really are and what we are capable of, because we are pushed to our limits and forced to confront our weaknesses and deepest desires.
So I started learning life coaching in order to lead myself, clear my mind, eliminate the fear and self-doubt, set new goals, and be consistent, focusing only on what I can control, and 1 year later... I successfully disciplined myself, into a better person and since then I have been helping others in similar cases."Life Coach"

Summer of 2022, I've been delving into the fascinating world of computer science and programming, embarking on a new learning adventure. This pursuit has opened up exciting new possibilities and has brought me to where I am today, like this website, I built entirely from scratch. "Programmer"
It's 7 years all together of self-taught, discipline and consistency.
The fusion of my passions and unimaginable journey, from dance to life coaching to programming, has shaped me into a better version of myself and I'm excited to see where this multifaceted path takes me in the future.
